# Ideal Customer Profile, the KLIQ coach

Who the answers are written for and who the platform serves. The Question Definer
uses this to decide whether a question is on-strategy.

## Primary ICP

Coaches and creators who run, or want to run, a paid community and content
business. They monetise expertise through memberships, courses, and a community.

## What they are trying to do

- Launch a community and get the first members active.
- Keep members engaged past the first week (the retention cliff).
- Price and package their offer.
- Grow recurring revenue (GMV) and move up the Growth Ladder.

## What they struggle with

- Members who join and go quiet (see `eda-post-once`).
- No idea what to publish or how often, especially in Week 1.
- Pricing with no reference point.
- Treating the community as broadcast rather than conversation.

## How we map them to the Growth Ladder

| Stage | Who they are |
| --- | --- |
| **launch** | Setting up, recruiting first members, posting first content. |
| **grow** | Have members, fighting the activation and retention cliff. |
| **monetise** | Turning engagement into revenue, first GMV milestones, pricing. |
| **scale** | Durable revenue, systematising, higher tiers. |
