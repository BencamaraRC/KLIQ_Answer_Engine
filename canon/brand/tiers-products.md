## Key definitions

- **GMV** gross merchandise value, the total a coach transacts through KLIQ.
  Milestones ($100, £5,000) are referenced by canon facts `ret-100gmv` and
  `ret-5000gmv`.
- **Activation** a new member taking the first meaningful action (posting,
  engaging with content) rather than just joining.
- **Golden window** the early period (Week 1) where engagement most strongly
  predicts retention. See `week1-content-engagement`.
